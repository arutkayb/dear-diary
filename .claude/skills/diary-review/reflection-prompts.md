# Reflection Question Bank

Use these questions to guide the iterative reflection conversation. Select 2-3 per round based on the day's activity shape. Ask specific versions — reference actual project names, features, or bugs from the diary.

---

## Accomplishments & Impact

Surface questions (start here, round 1):
- What did you actually ship or complete today — not what you worked on, but what's done?
- Which task from today do you feel best about, and why?

Deeper questions (round 2+):
- If you had to describe today's work to someone outside tech, what's the one sentence that captures it?
- What did you move forward that felt stuck before today?

---

## Learning & Growth

Surface questions:
- Did you learn anything today — a tool, a pattern, a shortcut — that you'll use again?
- What surprised you in a good way?

Deeper questions:
- Was there a moment where you had to stop and reconsider your approach? What triggered that?
- What assumption got challenged or proven wrong today?
- If you wrote a tweet-length note to your future self about today, what would it say?

---

## Challenges & Blockers

Surface questions:
- What was the hardest part of today, and how did you get through it (or not)?
- Was there anything you started but didn't finish? What got in the way?

Deeper questions:
- If you ran into a bug or unexpected behavior — what did it teach you about the system?
- What would you do differently if you faced the same problem tomorrow?

---

## Patterns & Workflow

Surface questions:
- Did today feel productive? What made it feel that way (or not)?
- Did you context-switch a lot today? How did that affect your focus?

Deeper questions:
- Is there a recurring type of work you keep doing that could be automated or templated?
- What was the ratio of "building new things" to "fixing/maintaining existing things" — and does that feel right?
- Did any tool or process slow you down in a way that feels fixable?

---

## Direction & Intent

Deeper questions (best for round 3 or after rich answers in earlier rounds):
- Is today's work moving toward something bigger? What's that bigger thing?
- What would you want to have shipped or figured out by end of week?
- If you zoomed out — what theme or thread connects what you worked on today?

---

## When to Use Which Questions

| Day shape | Recommended focus |
|-----------|------------------|
| Many projects (3+) | Patterns & Workflow → ask about context-switching and prioritization |
| Single deep project | Learning & Growth → ask about what was discovered or challenged |
| Bug-heavy day | Challenges & Blockers → ask about root cause insights and system understanding |
| Feature shipped | Accomplishments & Impact → ask about the "what's done" and who it helps |
| Lots of planning/tooling | Direction & Intent → ask about the bigger thread and what this enables |
| Mix of everything | Start with Accomplishments, then Learning, skip Patterns |
